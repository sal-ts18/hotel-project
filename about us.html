Welcome to Luxury Stay

Tucked away in the lush heart of southern Spain, Luxury Stay opened its doors in 2018 — born from a vision to create more than just a hotel, but a peaceful sanctuary where elegance meets nature. ur architecture blends modern comfort with timeless Mediterranean charm: wooden balconies shaded by tall trees, natural stone paths winding through gardens, and an inviting turquoise pool reflecting the clear Spanish skies. From the moment you arrive, time seems to slow down — and that’s exactly how we want it to feel.

Each of our rooms is inspired by Spanish artists and poets — from Lorca to Miró — reminding guests that beauty lives in every detail. Inside, hand-crafted tiles, soft linen, and subtle textures offer a sense of calm and authenticity. But the true soul of Luxury Stay is in the experience. Here, mornings begin with the scent of fresh rosemary bread and orange blossoms. Afternoons are spent poolside, beneath the rustling shade of old oaks. And in the evenings, you might hear the quiet echoes of history — this land once served as a retreat for writers and dreamers in the early 20th century, and that poetic spirit still lingers in the air.

Whether you're here to explore, rest, or reconnect, Luxury Stay offers something rare: a place that feels both new and timeless — luxurious, yet grounded. A place where every moment feels like it matters.
